Wordle

Following steps of the SDLC, my team of 5 developed a mock up version of the New York Times "Wordle." Over the course of 16 weeks, we made PBIs to work on during sprints, assigned scrum master, and held appropriate meetings like the sprint review and the sprint retrospective.
